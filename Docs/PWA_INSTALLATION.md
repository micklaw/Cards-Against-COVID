# Installing Cards Against COVID as an App

Cards Against COVID can be installed as a Progressive Web App (PWA) on your iOS or Android device, giving you a native app-like experience without browser toolbars.

## Installation Instructions

### iOS (iPhone/iPad) - Safari

1. Open Safari and navigate to the Cards Against COVID website
2. Tap the **Share** button (square with arrow pointing up) at the bottom of the screen
3. Scroll down and tap **"Add to Home Screen"**
4. (Optional) Edit the name if desired
5. Tap **"Add"** in the top right corner
6. The app icon will appear on your home screen

When you launch the app from your home screen, it will open in full-screen mode without the Safari browser interface.

### Android - Chrome

1. Open Chrome and navigate to the Cards Against COVID website
2. Tap the **three-dot menu** (â‹®) in the top right corner

When you launch the app from your home screen, it will open as a standalone app without the Chrome browser interface.

## Features

- **Standalone Mode**: No browser UI, just the game
- **App Icon**: Beautiful branded icon on your home screen
- **Quick Access**: Launch directly from home screen like any other app
- **Same Functionality**: All features work exactly as in the browser

## Technical Details

This is a Progressive Web App (PWA), which means:
- No App Store or Google Play Store installation required
- No large download - just add to home screen
- Always up-to-date (no manual updates needed)
- Works entirely in your web browser
- Requires internet connection to play (no offline mode)

## Troubleshooting

**iOS: "Add to Home Screen" option not showing?**
- Make sure you're using Safari (not Chrome or other browsers)
- Try refreshing the page and waiting a few seconds
- Ensure you're on iOS 11.3 or later

**Android: "Add to Home screen" option not showing?**
- Make sure you're using Chrome (recommended)
- Try refreshing the page and waiting a few seconds
- Ensure you have Chrome 76 or later
- Some Android browsers may not support PWAs

**Icon not appearing correctly?**
- Try removing the app and adding it again
- Clear your browser cache and try again
